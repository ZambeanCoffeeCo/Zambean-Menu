Zambean Coffee Company

Our Story

Born from a love for the rich, bold flavors of Zambian coffee, Zambean Coffee Company is a celebration of our heritage. We source the finest beans and pair them with fresh, soul-warming food to create a space where community and culture meet over a perfect cup.
